How It Works

Request

Request a divination from a qualified practitioner, select prayers from our extensive prayer library, or share your issues with our trained AI to get the most suitable prayer suggestions.

Connect

Confirm your prayers in one-click using any major payment method, and we’ll facilitate your request to a partner monastery to perform your prayers the very next day.

Experience

Join the monks performing your prayers LIVE online, have your name placed next to the altar, and receive prayer photos along with a completion certificate.
